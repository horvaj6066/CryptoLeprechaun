Machine Learning with an Recurrent Neural Network

I applied a specific type of recurrent neural network (RNN) to forecast Bitcoin price. This type is called a Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) and has feedback loops in order to be able to apply predictions to a time-series. Please click on the the Project Info drop down menu in the top right for more information on the model and parameters that I used.

Visualizing the Profitability of Our Prediction Model

In the Candlestick chart, the purple horizontal lines are the price predictions made each day by the machine learning model. The bar chart below the Candlestick chart show the daily profitability (PnL) from buying or shorting one Bitcoin each day based on our model's price prediction. There is just over a hundred price predictions based on the test data and we add to this each day with one live prediction at 5PM PT. Use the date slider above the Candlestick chart to isolate date ranges of interest and this will also update the hit rate and profitability summary statistics.
